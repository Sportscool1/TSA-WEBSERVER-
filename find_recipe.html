Easy Pancakes

Fluffy and delicious pancakes that are quick to make!

See Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk

Steps:

  • Mix dry ingredients together.
  • Add wet ingredients and stir until smooth.
  • Cook on a greased pan until golden brown.

Chicken Stir Fry

A tasty and colorful stir fry with simple ingredients.

See Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps:

  • Slice the chicken and vegetables.
  • Heat oil in a pan and cook chicken.
  • Add vegetables and soy sauce, stir-fry until cooked.
