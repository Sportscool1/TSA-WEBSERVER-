Easy Pancakes

Fluffy and delicious pancakes that are quick to make!

See Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add milk and eggs, whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan, pour batter, and cook until golden brown.
  4. Flip and cook the other side.

Chicken Stir Fry

A tasty and colorful stir fry with simple ingredients.

See Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ginger

Steps

  1. Slice chicken thinly and season with soy sauce.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, cook chicken until browned.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 5 minutes.
  4. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
